The journey we’ve taken, crossing countless mountains and rivers.

  • 2023

    Where We Begin

    Australia’s housing crisis deepens with skyrocketing prices, surging rents, and rising homelessness. Construction companies are collapsing, interest rates hit a 20-year high, and mortgage pressures are overwhelming. New housing supply lags far behind demand, leaving no easy solutions in sight.

  • 2024

    Taking action

    We’re turning to technology and focusing on efficiency. Fabricated manufacturing replaces on-site construction. Modular options take the place of fully customized designs. Supply chain collaboration replaces reliance on subcontractors. After relentless trial and effort, hope is finally within sight.
